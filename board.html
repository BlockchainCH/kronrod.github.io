Luzius Meisser President

Email - Twitter
Luzius holds a Master in Computer Science from ETH and is currently pursuing a second Master in Economics at the University of Zurich. In between, he founded secure online storage Wuala, which was acquired in 2009 by LaCie and which he left in early 2013. He also teaches object oriented programming at the university of applied sciences and arts (FHNW) in Brugg. Before he found out about Bitcoin in early 2011, he had serious doubts whether it is even possible to implement a decentralized currency like Bitcoin. He summarized his views and hopes regarding Bitcoin in an article which was published as chapter of the book Next Generation Finance which you can read here.

Alexis Roussel Vice President

Email - Twitter
Alexis holds a Master in New Technology public law from Paris 10 university. He served as E-Governance specialist for the United Nations. He participated in the World Summit on the Information Society in 2003 and 2005. He joined RWE Switzerland in 2008, an energy trading company, as one of the managers of the Back-Office. He also joined the Pirate Party of Switzerland to promote a human-centric and distributed approach of a technological society. Since 2013, he is the President of the Pirate Party. He is currently working on a financial project including Bitcoins.

Stefan Greiner Secretary

Stefan is a lawyer specialized on international business law. He has worked for law firms in Germany, Singapore and Japan before he became General Counsel of a Swiss holding company with world-wide operations. He is currently working for a global e-commerce enterprise and writing a PhD thesis about the legal aspects of digital currencies at the University of Zurich.
