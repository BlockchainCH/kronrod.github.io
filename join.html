FAQ

How do I become a member?

You can apply for membership by filling in the form below and paying your yearly membership fee.

Who is allowed to join?

Anyone can join our association - legal entities as well as natural persons. However, when voting, we only count votes of members residing in Switzerland.

What is the yearly membership fee?

As Bitcoin is still quite volatile, we chose to leave the amount with which you want to support us up to you. In order to run the organization, the equivalent of 50 CHF per member would be great. The minimum necessary to become a member is 1 Satoshi per year. Sending us 1 BTC will make you a lifetime member.

When does my membership become valid?

As soon as we have reviewed your application and sent you the confirmation. This can take a few business days.
